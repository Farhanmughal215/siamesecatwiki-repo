# siamese.wiki — article roadmap

The 30-article launch plan, ordered by SEO priority and topical coverage. Articles are grouped into five pillars; we publish in numerical order unless a reviewer reprioritizes.

**Target cadence:** 2 articles/week for the first 15 weeks, then quarterly refresh.

**Success metric for launch batch (articles #01–#15):** rank on page 1 of Google for at least 8 of the primary keywords within 6 months.

---

## How to read this roadmap

Each article entry contains:

- **#** — article number (matches filenames: `/outlines/07-seal-point-siamese-guide.md`)
- **Title** — working H1. Writer can tighten for keyword placement.
- **Slug** — URL-safe, lowercase, hyphenated
- **Primary keyword** — the one Google query this article is built to answer
- **Category** — pillar (see §1 below)
- **CTA tier** — Loud / Medium / Subtle (per CLAUDE.md §5)
- **Target words** — writer's length target, ±15%
- **Priority** — P0 (launch critical) / P1 (tier 2) / P2 (fills out coverage)
- **Notes** — any angle, positioning, or research hints

## 4 · Review gates

No article publishes without all three gates cleared:

1. **Factual gate** — every non-trivial claim has a cited source per CLAUDE.md §7. Reviewer spot-checks 3 random claims per article against their sources.
2. **Voice gate** — reviewer reads top-to-bottom. If any sentence sounds AI-generated, rewrite it. No exceptions.
3. **CTA gate** — CTA tier matches the table in CLAUDE.md §5. Health articles get footer-only. No deep links to café sub-pages.
